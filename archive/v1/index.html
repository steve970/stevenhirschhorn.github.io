About Me

My life is one big adventure. Whether it is growing my own food, attempting to climb a mountain, or learning a new programming language, I approach everything with an open mind, a willingness to learn and grow from my experiences, and an overall appreciation for life. I'm a fullstack developer with a background in marketing and international business from the University of Colorado. My professional experience includes all facets of marketing, farm management, account/project management, and restaurant management. Since pivoting from my marketing career, I completed the Galvanize Full Stack development program and I have been focused primarily in JavaScript and Ruby. I have been exposed to many different technologies and frameworks and am always tinkering around on something in my free time. I like to think I bring a lot to the table. Consider me a fullstack professional.
